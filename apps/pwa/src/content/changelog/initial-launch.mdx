# Initial Launch of Cebby

We're thrilled to announce the official launch of Cebby, a dedicated platform for the Cebu tech community to discover and engage with local tech events!

## Key Features

- **Event Discovery**: Find all tech events happening in Cebu in one place
- **Calendar Integration**: Easily add events to your personal calendar
- **Mobile-Friendly Design**: Access Cebby on any device with our responsive PWA

## What's Next?

We're just getting started! Our roadmap includes:

- Enhanced filtering options
- User accounts and personalization
- Event recommendations based on your interests
- Integration with more event platforms

Thank you for joining us on this journey to connect and strengthen the Cebu tech community!
